# OnTheGo: Business & Revenue Model

OnTheGo focuses on a high-volume, low-margin transactional model supplemented by recurring subscription revenue.

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Transactional Success Fee
- **Model:** A 10-15% service fee applied to the total cost-sharing contribution for each ride.
- **Value:** Covers the insurance, safety features, and platform maintenance costs.

### 2. Commuter Subscription (OnTheGo Pass)
- **Model:** A monthly fee (e.g., ₹299/month) for frequent users.
- **Benefits:** 
    - Zero success fees.
    - Priority matching in high-demand zones.
    - Enhanced safety features (e.g., "Safe-Home" live monitoring).

### 3. Corporate Mobility as a Service (CMaaS)
- **Model:** B2B contracts with companies to facilitate staff carpooling.
- **Value:** Companies reduce parking space requirements and meet sustainability targets (ESG reporting).
- **Revenue:** Tiered pricing based on the number of active employees.

### 4. Strategic Partnerships & API Licensing
- **Model:** Licensing the "Live Drive" technology to logistics or delivery companies to optimize their routes using shared passenger/delivery models.

## Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Phase 1: High-Density Corridors
We will target specific routes with predictable daily traffic (e.g., Gurgaon to Delhi, Hisar to Chandigarh).

### Phase 2: Hyper-Local Community Growth
- **Referral Programs:** Incentivizing drivers to bring their existing carpooling friends onto the platform for better tracking and security.
- **Campus Launches:** Partnerships with University and Office hubs to create initial density.

### Phase 3: Strategic Discounts
Early-adopter incentives for "Early Bird" drivers who commit to activating the mode during peak hours for the first 30 days.

## Projected Growth Path
- **Month 1-6:** Pilot launch, refining the matching engine, 500+ active daily users.
- **Month 6-12:** Expansion to 3 more cities, launching the corporate module.
- **Year 2+:** Nationwide scaling and introduction of value-added services (e.g., parcel sharing).

---
*The goal is to maintain a healthy unit economic structure where user acquisition costs (CAC) are recouped within the first 3 months of active use.*
